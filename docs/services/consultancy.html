OUR SERVICES

Technology Consultancy

Independent technical advice from senior developers who’ve made these decisions before – and learned which trade-offs actually matter when the slides are switched off.

If one of these sounds familiar, we can help

You’ve inherited tech you don’t fully understand

Maybe the original team has gone. Maybe the documentation never existed. You’ve got software running the business, and a sense that change is harder than it should be – but no clear picture of what’s actually under the hood. We’ll review what’s there and tell you what’s healthy, what’s risky, and what’s genuinely slowing you down – without the conflict of interest of also wanting to sell you a rebuild.

You’re about to make a big technology call

Build or buy. SaaS or bespoke. Migrate to the cloud or stay where you are. Add AI or hold back. We’ve sat in many of these conversations and can tell you what we’ve seen work, what we’ve seen fail, and what the trade-offs actually look like once the marketing is stripped away.

Your team is busy but the wrong things keep shipping

The developers are working hard. The roadmap looks reasonable. But the business doesn’t feel like it’s getting what it needs. We’ll look at how the team works, what’s getting in their way, and where small changes give you back time and confidence – through code review, architecture review, or process changes.

Where we add value

  • Architecture and design review
  • Code review and quality assessment
  • Build-vs-buy and supplier evaluation
  • Cloud and data strategy
  • Software re-engineering and migration planning
  • Senior leadership advisory and workshops

We’ll tell you upfront if we’re not the right fit. If you need impartial advice with total discretion, please get in touch.
