OUR SERVICES

Technology Consultancy

With the almost constant release of new applications, platforms and technical innovations, making the right choices about what to adopt is becoming increasingly critical. With our technical expertise and business experience Elastic Mint will help you adopt the best solution. Most importantly if we do not feel we have sufficient expertise in a particular field we will tell you at the outset.

Our teams highly experienced developers work closely with our clients’ internal IT and business leads

There are many aspects of technology consultancy that we cover but these are the main ones:

  • Software architecture and design
  • Usability and interaction design
  • Testing and quality assurance
  • Technical and user documentation
  • Software re-engineering and migration
  • Data storage and management
  • Code evaluation and review
  • Senior Executive technology specification and software development project management training

Our clients hire us because we are:

  • Totally independent
  • Vastly experienced
  • Honest in our assessment
  • Adept at identifying complex business and technology issues
  • Able to provide and suggest innovative solutions when required
  • A personal bespoke service tailored to your requirements

 

If you need impartial advice with total discretion please get in touch.
