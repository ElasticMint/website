Tech Leadership

The increasingly global nature of business in general and our client base in particular means that our clients' software development teams and business leads may be based in multiple locations either nationally or internationally.

Our teams of highly experienced developers work closely the with our clients’ internal IT and business leads

The nature of the software development work that Elastic Mint undertakes means that we are often called upon to lead teams whose members may be across multiple time zones. In addition English may not be their first language, there may be differing working and cultural norms as well as differing skill set and skill levels across the team. This adds an additional layer of challenge and complexity to what may already be a complex software development programme.

Elastic Mint is experienced and adept at managing and leading distributed teams of this nature and in supporting senior executives tasked with this role. We take full advantage of tools such as Teams and Zoom to enable us to rapidly communicate and build up close, effective working relationships. We follow Agile processes such as daily standups and regular retrospectives to provide a structure, thus helping us communicate efficiently and in a timely manner. We make time for regular catchups with individuals to ensure high morale and to share values and ways of working.
