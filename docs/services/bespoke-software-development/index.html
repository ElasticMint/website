Bespoke Software Development

Why do global technology behemoths, technology start-ups and SMEs of all shapes and sizes come to Elastic Mint, based in Bristol in the first place?  A quick search on Google will bring up a whole raft of larger, possibly cheaper, more global alternatives who claim to be equally accomplished.

Our clients tell us it comes down to 4 very important factors, which we list below in no particular order.

  1. Our developers are highly experienced who, in addition to being exceptional developers, are more than capable of leading teams of developers and working with senior business leaders to ensure the desired business and technical requirements are met on time and in full – see case studies
  2. We work in a transparent way, so there are no nasty surprises
  3. We are very flexible in the way we work so if business needs or technical requirements change we are prepared for that and adapt accordingly
  4. Good or bad we always tell the truth and work in our clients best interest

Most of our software development work falls into four categories:

  1. Facilitating operational performance improvement e.g. improving customer service, streamlining and automating business processes, managing data to improve business insight and value, helping teams of developers to deliver projects faster and with greater success
  2. Enabling business change by introducing new products and services,  enabling collaboration in new ways on a global basis
  3. Supplementing client internal software development capability by providing a developer or teams of developers with the specific skills and experience required
  4. Software development resources on tap i.e. purchasing a number of man days that can be called on when required

Bespoke software applications might encompass:

  • Customer portals
  • Intranets
  • Databases
  • ERP
  • CRM
  • Process automation
  • Dashboards
  • Client self-service solutions
  • Reports and self-service data
  • System migration

We predominantly use Microsoft technologies such as C#, .Net Core, SQL Server and Azure to build systems comprising web-based applications and APIs. If the project is outside our expertise we will say so at the outset.
