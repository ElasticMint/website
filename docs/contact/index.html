Contact

Get in touch

If you’d like a free consultation or have any other queries, book a meeting or send us an email.

We’re a remote team working with clients across the UK and internationally, offering bespoke software development, technology consultancy, distributed team leadership and fractional CTO services.

Whether you have a defined project in mind, a problem you’re not yet sure how to solve, or simply want to find out whether Elastic Mint might be a good fit for your organisation, we welcome the conversation. We’ll give you an honest assessment of whether and how we can help.

Your first call is just a conversation. We’ll listen to what you’re trying to do, ask questions to understand the constraints, and only suggest a way forward if we’re confident we can help. There’s no hard sell, and we’re happy to sign an NDA if you want to discuss anything sensitive.

We’re happy to hear from you whether you have a defined project, are weighing up a tech decision and want a second opinion, are considering a fractional CTO arrangement, or need extra engineering capacity alongside your existing team. The earlier we’re brought into the conversation, the more useful we tend to be.

We aim to respond to all enquiries within two working days.
